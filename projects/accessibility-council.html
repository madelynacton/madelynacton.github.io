Serve IT: Web Accessibility Council

A community-engaged accessibility audit project focused on improving website usability and inclusion for local businesses.

Web Accessibility Council Poster (PDF)

Objective

The objective of this project was to evaluate and improve the accessibility of local business websites in partnership with the Bloomington Council for Community Accessibility and the City of Bloomington. As part of the Serve IT Accessibility Council, our team reviewed websites to identify barriers that could impact users with cognitive, visual, or hearing disabilities. The goal was to provide clear, actionable recommendations that would help make web content more inclusive, usable, and compliant with accessibility best practices.

Implementation

Throughout the Fall 2023 semester, my team and I met weekly to review local business websites, including restaurants, retail stores, and service providers. We conducted accessibility audits using industry-standard tools such as WAVE (Web Accessibility Evaluation Tool) and Axe DevTools to assess issues related to color contrast, keyboard navigation, content structure, image and video alt text, and interactive components such as carousels.

For each website, we completed a series of structured surveys provided by our client, documenting accessibility issues and recommending specific improvements. These surveys were then submitted to the City of Bloomington for review and follow-up. Midway through the semester, our team identified a gap in our workflow and adjusted our process to ensure that all required surveys were completed for every website evaluated. By adapting quickly, we successfully reviewed and submitted accessibility recommendations for over 15 local businesses.

Results

This project significantly expanded my understanding of web accessibility and inclusive design. Starting with limited prior knowledge, I gained hands-on experience identifying accessibility issues and evaluating websites through the lens of diverse user needs. The experience reinforced the importance of designing digital experiences that are usable by everyone and strengthened my technical and analytical skills related to accessibility auditing and UX evaluation.

  • Reviewed and provided accessibility recommendations for 15+ local business websites
  • Used WAVE and Axe DevTools to identify accessibility issues and usability barriers
  • Documented findings through structured client-facing surveys
  • Developed a stronger foundation in inclusive, user-centered web design practices
