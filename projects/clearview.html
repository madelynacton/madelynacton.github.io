Clearview

An accessibility-focused prototype developed through user research and design thinking to support visually impaired users in reading printed text.

Prototype Project Portfolio (PDF)

Objective

My team followed the design thinking double diamond process, emphasizing discovery and definition before moving into ideation and prototyping. We conducted user interviews, analyzed accessibility research, and synthesized findings to uncover a gap in existing assistive technologies. Based on these insights, we designed a low-fidelity website prototype for Clearview, focusing on a smart glasses text-scanning feature that reads content aloud, along with supporting accessibility-driven interface decisions.

Implementation

My team followed the design thinking double diamond process, emphasizing discovery and definition before moving into ideation and prototyping. We conducted user interviews, analyzed accessibility research, and synthesized findings to uncover a gap in existing assistive technologies. Based on these insights, we designed a low-fidelity website prototype for Clearview, focusing on a text-scanning feature for smart glasses that reads content aloud, along with supporting accessibility-driven interface decisions.

Results

The final prototype demonstrated how research-driven design can lead to practical, user-centered accessibility solutions. Clearview successfully communicated its core functionality through a low-fidelity interface while grounding design decisions in real user needs. The project highlighted the value of structured design frameworks when tackling complex, human-centered problems.

  • Applied the design thinking double diamond framework from research to final concept
  • Conducted and synthesized primary user interviews and secondary research
  • Identified a real accessibility gap and translated insights into design decisions
  • Designed a low-fidelity prototype centered on assistive technology principles
  • Strengthened skills in human-centered design, collaboration, and design communication
